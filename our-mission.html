Statistics

  1. Approximately 7.6 million companion animals enter animal shelters nationwide every year. Of those, approximately 3.4 million are cats.
  2. Each year, approximately 2.7 million animals are euthanized (1.4 million cats).
  3. Approximately 2.7 million shelter animals are adopted each year (1.3 million cats).
  4. About 649,000 animals who enter shelters as strays are returned to their owners. Of those, only 100,000 are cats.
